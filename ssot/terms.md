# Terms

## SSOT
Single Source of Truth; the authoritative set of documents that define the system.

## GENESIS
The initial phase where the foundational SSOT is defined.

## ARCHIVE
Reference-only materials that are retained but not active SSOT.

## STAGING
Drafts and proposals that may become SSOT.

## MVP
Minimum viable product; the smallest working system that meets the current goal.

## Kill-switch
A deterministic mechanism that halts trading activity when risk limits are breached.

## AccountSnapshot
A point-in-time record of account state including balances, equity, margin usage, and position counts.
